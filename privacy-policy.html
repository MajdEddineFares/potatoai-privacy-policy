Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle and protect your personal data while using our chatbot services. We prioritize your privacy and are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of any information shared with our chatbot.

Information Collection

Our chatbot interacts with users and may collect certain data, such as the content of your queries or interactions. The data collected is used solely for the purpose of providing responses and improving the chatbot's accuracy and performance.

We do not collect any personal identification information (such as name, email, or contact details) unless explicitly provided by the user.

Data Usage

The data collected by our chatbot is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Enhancing the chatbot's understanding and improving the accuracy of its responses.
  • Providing users with relevant and accurate information based on their queries.

Confidentiality and Data Access

We take strict measures to ensure the confidentiality of your data:

  • No one has access to the data: The data shared with our chatbot is processed in a secure environment, and no external party or individual has access to it.
  • No human intervention: All responses provided by the chatbot are generated by machine learning models. No human oversight is involved in handling or viewing the data.

Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. These measures include encryption and secure storage protocols that ensure your data is handled with the highest level of security.

Data Retention

We do not retain any user data for longer than is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above. Once the interaction with the chatbot concludes, the data is securely deleted from our systems.

Your Rights

As a user of this chatbot, you have the following rights:

  • The right to request the deletion of any personal data shared with the chatbot (if any).
  • The right to inquire about the types of data collected during interactions.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or our operations. Any significant changes will be communicated to users through an updated version of this policy.
